Our Story

We believe in shared knowledge and the power behind it. Many local businesses strive to be the best in the field they're in, but the journey is never simple. Nobody said it will be at least.

We believe that learning from each other and growing together can help us be successfull in whatever business we're about.

RuslterX was born to address challenges faced by local businesses. Our research has shown us that, one of the key issues faced by small businesses is visibility and sustainability. We have developed this platform as a way to bridge a gap between small and big businesses.

This platform will help local businesses with the following:

  • Visibility.
  • Free tool available to them.
  • Fire Stories from other entrepreneurs.
